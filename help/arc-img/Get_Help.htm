ASK A LIBRARIAN

Stop by the Research Help Desk whenever the library is open to speak with a librarian.
Call the Research Help Desk at (916) 484-8458.
Text your library questions to (916) 604-8976. We'll respond as soon as possible. This service is staffed M-F, 9am - 5pm.
Email us your reference questions.
Research Appointments are one-on-one, in-person sessions with a librarian. Use the Research Appointment Calendar to schedule your appointment. Note that research appointments must be made at least two business days in advance.

Chat with a librarian. This service is staffed as often as possible M-F, 9am - 5pm.
